Soapstone

Born out of a love for Fromsoft’s Dark Souls game series, Soapstone is a mobile app that allows fans of the game to replicate one of the game’s most famous and unique features in the real world. This project was featured on Kotaku, PC Gamer, and a handful of other gaming news sites. Work on Soapstone continues, and a new version will be available with lots of new features to coincide with the release of Dark Souls III in April 2016.

  • Ionic Framework
  • Custom Cordova plugin for background geolocation
  • MEAN backend
  • RESTful API for app queries
  • Over 42k users

Snapta

A first attempt at entrepreneurship, Snapta was designed to be a way for Reno’s entertainment venues to sell unsold seats at the last minute at a discount. The associated Android and iOS mobile apps received push notifications when a deal was available, and allowed a user to purchase a ticket quickly and easily.

After many successful pitch meetings with local venues such as the Silver Legacy, Reno Bighorns, and the Grand Sierra Resort, we had several clients lined up to get started. Unfortunately, they found themselves at the mercy of their exclusive contracts with the behemoth that is Ticketmaster.

  • LAMP stack
  • Fully functional CC processing
  • Associated native mobile app
